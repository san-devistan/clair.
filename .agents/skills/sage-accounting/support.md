Overview
The Developer Services team provides support for professionals working with the Sage Business Cloud Accounting API. We provide a wide range of self-serve documentation and the developer community for peer-to-peer support, but we understand that from time to time, you might need a little bit of extra help from one of our team.
The Developer Services team is UK based and support is available via email Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm excluding UK public holidays.
Please contact the team by email at sbc-accounting-api-developers@sage.com .
